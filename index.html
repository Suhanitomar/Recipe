Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes
Ingredients
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
Instructions
  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Cook the omlette
  4. Add fillings(optional)
  5. Fold and serve
  6. Enjoy
